Sky Gate

The movie is base on a semi-fairy-tail futuristic story book.

Tenth of years from now, there was a economic crisis on earth. The bank systems were locked down without any reason. The entrepreneurs found a very straight cipher code left inside the systems. Then, many people were disappeared and were brought to a new secret planet. Apocalypse started on the earth, many people died. The others were drained and became machine experimental subjects.

The story began many year after the crisis period. The young teenagers were now living in an ancient technology and historical city, where they struggling to live day by day, apart from their parents. Another reason was from the political system. But another, the most important reason, was from their own life. With their dreams, hunger and wisdom, they together have to find the way to unlock the cipher behind the history and their life and fight against the threats from the space. Those who were finding us and ready to wipe out all the human race.
